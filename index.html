What I Do

Offensive Security Advisory

Not a Pentest. Not an Audit.

I provide Offensive Security Advisory for organizations that need clarity on their real security risk. This is a technical consultation through an attacker's lens: mapping your attack surface, running essential scans, and translating findings into prioritized business risk.

You get a clear picture of what an attacker sees, what's actually exposed, and how to treat it. No compliance checklists. No full engagement overhead. Just actionable guidance from someone who has led hundreds of assessments across banking, telecom, and fintech.

This is for organizations unsure of their actual risk exposure, teams preparing for or recovering from a security engagement, and decision-makers who need actionable guidance — not a technical report.
