Not a Pentest. Not an Audit.

I provide Offensive Security Advisory for organizations that need clarity on their real security risk — fast. Problem Solving

How I Operate Under Pressure

01

Building Structure Where There Was None

When I joined Inovasys, offensive security delivery was unstructured and reactive. No standardized process, no governance, no visibility. I co-designed an end-to-end delivery framework with the PMO — covering scoping, execution, QA, reporting, and closure. Introduced OKRs, KPIs, dashboards, and governance cadences. The result was a predictable, measurable delivery engine handling 30+ engagements monthly.

02

Delivering Under Crisis

A group resignation left the team at 25% of its original capacity with active client commitments in flight. Rather than escalating or missing deliverables, I restructured the workload, delegated strategically, and empowered juniors and mid-levels to operate above their typical level. The team maintained 100% delivery continuity — zero revenue loss, zero client loss — while simultaneously onboarding new hires who became billable within 2 months.
